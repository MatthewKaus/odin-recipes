Spam Musubi

Ingredients

  • 12oz Spam
  • Soy Sauce
  • Rice
  • Sea Weed

Steps

  1. Cook the rice either on a stovetop, or rice cooker.
  2. Slice the spam into about 8-10 slices depending on the thickness you would like.
  3. Place the sliced spam into a skillet and fry until the desired crispness.
  4. With the spam tin, add the seaweed wrap into the tin, along with the spam slice and pack down with the cooked rice
  5. After packing down, remove the Spam Musubi from the tin, and add the desired amount of soy sauce.
