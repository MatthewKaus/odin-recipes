Oyakudon

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 1/2 onion
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup dashi
  • 1 1/2 Tbsp Mirin
  • 1 1/2 Tbsp Sake
  • 1 1/2 Tbsp Soy Sauce
  • 1 1/2 tsp sugar

Steps

  1. Combine Dashi, mirin, sake, and soy sauce into a bowl
  2. Add the sugar mix.
  3. Chop the onion into 2mm slices
  4. Slice the chicken thighs into 1/2 thick squares
  5. Saute the onions with the sauce mixture
  6. Add the chicken and bring to a boil
  7. Once boiling, cover until the chicken is no longer pink
  8. Slowly add the beaten eggs into the mix
  9. Add the green onion after cooked, and serve.
