Gyudon

Ingredients

  • Thinly Sliced Beef
  • 1/2 onion
  • 1 1/2 Tbsp Mirin
  • 1 1/2 Tbsp Sake
  • 1 1/2 Tbsp Soy Sauce
  • 1 1/2 tsp sugar

Steps

  1. Combine Dashi, mirin, sake, and soysauce into a bowl
  2. Add the sugar to the mix
  3. Chop onion into 2mm slices
  4. Saute the onions with the sauce mixture
  5. Add the thin slices of beef
  6. Cook until browned
  7. Add more Dashi to the mix for a soup consitancy
  8. Add Green onion to the to the pan
  9. When finished, add beef and onions to rice bowl
  10. Serve
